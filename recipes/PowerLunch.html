PowerLunch

The Fight Club body lunch is crucial to keep your lean momentum going. Consistency is important to get the results you want!

    Ingredients
  • Chicken Breast
  • Brown Rice
  • Broccoli
    Directions
  1. Heat oven to 400 degrees. Place Chicken Breast in oven and bake for 40 minutes.
  2. Boil pot of water. Place rice in boiling water, reduce heat to simmer. Cover and let cook for 25 minutes.
  3. Place broccoli in 400 degree oven and let cook for 20 minutes.
