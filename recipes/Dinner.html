Lean Dinner

The Fight Club Dinner is the best way to finish your day feeling your best. Below is a great recipe to keep you looking good.

    Ingredients
  • Grilled Fish
  • Baked Potato
  • More Veggies!
    Directions
  1. Heat oven to 400 degrees. Place Fish of your choice in oven and bake for 45 minutes.
  2. Place Potato and Green Veggies of your choice (I prefer Asparagus) in a different oven rack while cooking fish
  3. Remove the potato and veggies after 40 minutes. Remove Fish after 45 minutes
  4. Plate Fish, Baked potato and Veggies. Enjoy!
