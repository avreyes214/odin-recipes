Fight Club Breakfast of Champs

The Fight Club diet starts at breakfast. This meal will have you feeling good without feeling too bloated. Follow the recipe below to reach your fitness goals.

    Ingredients
  • 6 Eggs or Egg Whites
  • 1 cup of spinach
  • 1 Banana
  • 1 Cup of Strawberries
  • 1 Cup of Blueberries
  • 1 Cup of Milk or Water
    Directions
  1. Using a pan or Microwave, cook egg whites to desired consistency.
  2. Place Spinach, Banana, Strawberries, Blueberries, Ice, Water or Milk into Blender. Blend until fully liquid.
  3. Add peanut butter to smoothie for extra flavoring
